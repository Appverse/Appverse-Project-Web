The Appverse Story

GFT Appverse is leading the way in open source, collaborative development with the creation of The Appverse Project. The project's goal is to invest greater resources into enabling and optimizing open-source software and facilitating open collaboration with our clients, users, and the development community as a whole. GFT Appverse will enable the open source community and special interest groups to more rapidly advance collaboration in an open-innovation context.
