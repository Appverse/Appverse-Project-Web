Start an application in 15 seconds with Appverse P15 ;)

Project in 15 (a.k.a P15) is the quick jump into Appverse platform. Its main objective is to help you starting quickly with your projects.


Currently it is on its germinal stage. It is just the first release supporting a small amount of customization options. We envision extending this to a wider platform with further customization options. Even we want to support developer community with its local development environment warm-up and deployment platforms provisioning.
